About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating sustainable social impact across India. Through initiatives in education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, skill development, and community welfare, the foundation strives to build stronger and more inclusive communities.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Food and clothing distribution initiative supporting underprivileged communities and promoting dignity and welfare.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Providing educational support, digital literacy, life skills training, and learning opportunities for children.

Project JEEV

Animal welfare initiative focused on feeding stray animals, providing care, and promoting compassion.

Project UDAAN

Women empowerment program encouraging entrepreneurship, leadership, financial independence, and skill development.

Project PRAKRITI

Environmental sustainability initiative promoting tree plantation drives and eco-friendly practices.

Project VIKAS

Skill development and internship opportunities for youth in various professional domains.
